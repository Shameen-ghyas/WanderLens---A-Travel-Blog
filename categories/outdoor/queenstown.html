The World's Adventure Playground

Nestled on the shores of Lake Wakatipu with a dramatic mountain backdrop, Queenstown is New Zealand's premier destination for adrenaline-fueled activities and breathtaking landscapes.

This South Island gem invented commercial bungee jumping and continues to push adventure tourism boundaries. From jet boating through narrow canyons to heli-skiing pristine slopes, Queenstown offers year-round thrills amidst some of the planet's most stunning scenery.

Beyond the adrenaline, Queenstown charms visitors with its vibrant lakeside dining scene, world-class vineyards, and access to Fiordland National Park's wilderness.

Quick Facts
  • Location: South Island, New Zealand
  • Best Time to Visit: Dec-Feb (summer), Jun-Aug (winter)
  • Elevation: 310 meters (1,017 ft)
  • Famous For: Bungee jumping, skiing, Lord of the Rings scenery
  • Signature Activity: Kawarau Bridge Bungee (43m)

Queenstown Adventures

Bungee Jumping

Leap from the historic Kawarau Bridge where commercial bungee began, or try the 134m Nevis Swing for the ultimate adrenaline rush.

Skiing & Snowboarding

Hit the slopes at Remarkables or Coronet Peak, with terrain for all levels and stunning alpine views.

Shotover Jet

Experience 85km/h spins through narrow canyons on this iconic whitewater jet boat ride.

Adventure Activities

Skydiving

Jump from 15,000ft over the Remarkables mountain range for breathtaking aerial views.

Whitewater Rafting

Navigate grade 4-5 rapids on the Shotover or Kawarau Rivers with expert guides.

Routeburn Track

Hike this world-famous 32km trail through Fiordland and Mount Aspiring National Parks.

Heli-Skiing

Access untouched powder runs via helicopter in the Southern Alps.

Queenstown Beauty

Essential Travel Tips

When to Visit

Summer (Dec-Feb) offers long days perfect for hiking and water sports. Winter (Jun-Aug) brings world-class skiing. Shoulder seasons provide fewer crowds and lower prices.

Adventure Planning

Book popular activities like bungee jumping or Milford Sound tours in advance. Many operators offer combo packages for multiple adventures.

Dining & Après

Don't miss Fergburger (iconic burgers), Eichardt's Bar for cocktails, and local wineries like Gibbston Valley.

Day Trips

Visit Arrowtown's historic gold mining village, tour Milford Sound, or explore Wanaka's stunning landscapes.
