Gateway to Canyon Country

Nestled between Arches and Canyonlands National Parks, Moab is the ultimate playground for desert adventurers, with its towering red rock formations, winding canyons, and the mighty Colorado River.

This small Utah town has become world-famous for its mountain biking trails, off-roading routes, and breathtaking national park scenery. The contrast of fiery red rocks against deep blue skies creates a landscape that feels both otherworldly and profoundly ancient.

From sunrise at Delicate Arch to sunset over Dead Horse Point, Moab offers endless opportunities for adventure and awe-inspiring moments in nature.

Quick Facts
  • Location: Eastern Utah, USA
  • Best Time to Visit: March-May & Sept-Oct
  • Elevation: 4,025 ft (1,227 m)
  • Famous For: Arches NP, mountain biking, jeep trails
  • Iconic Landmark: Delicate Arch

Moab Adventures

Arches National Park

Explore over 2,000 natural sandstone arches including the iconic Delicate Arch and Landscape Arch.

Mountain Biking

Ride world-famous trails like Slickrock and Porcupine Rim with stunning desert vistas.

Colorado River

Raft through Westwater Canyon or kayak the calm stretches near town.

Desert Activities

Hiking

Trek to Delicate Arch, explore Fiery Furnace, or discover hidden gems in Canyonlands.

4x4 Off-Roading

Conquer legendary trails like Hell's Revenge and Poison Spider Mesa.

Photography

Capture stunning red rock formations at golden hour and under starry skies.

Canyoneering

Rappel into slot canyons and discover hidden water pockets in the desert.

Moab Through My Lens

Essential Travel Tips

Weather & Timing

Spring and fall offer ideal temperatures. Summer can exceed 100°F - hike early and carry 3L+ water per person. Winter brings cool days and occasional snow.

Park Reservations

Arches requires timed entry tickets April-Oct. Arrive before 6am or after 4pm to avoid crowds at popular trails.

Local Eats

Try the Moab Brewery, Quesadilla Mobilla food truck, and Milt's Stop & Eat for classic burgers and shakes.

Nearby Adventures

Don't miss Dead Horse Point State Park, Corona Arch, and the Island in the Sky district of Canyonlands.
